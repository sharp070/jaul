About Us

This is a learning website made to aid the development of unique learners, specifically visual learners ranging from grades 7-9.

It was created by aspiring coders in hopes of winning a challenge, given at a code marathon, hosted by Coders of the Caribbean. We believe persons learn differently and a popular method of learning is visual learning.

Questions & Answers

  • "Who is a visual learner?"

    A visual learner is a person who learns best by use of visual aids, ie, they learn best by seeing what is taught.

  • "How does one identify a visual learner?"

    There are no hard or fast rules in identifying visual learners, some common characteristics may be:

    • Good spelling
    • Nice handwriting
    • Easy memorization
    • Organized
    • A watcher distracted by noise

  • "Why target grades 7-9?"

    The Caribbean is a test driven society. It was observed that another website targeted children in grades 6,10 & 11, these grades are considered the more important grades as it is where all the major testing is done. This leaves a gap where a lot of students miss out on the basic knowledge needed and it also adds a bonus where the competitive nature is kept at all stages.
